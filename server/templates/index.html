3Low-frequency character

The AC does not hum steadily, it kicks in. These bars show how far the level jumps from a quiet moment to a loud compressor onset. The low-frequency (dBC) jump is the larger one, and low rumble passes through walls most easily, so it is the hardest to sleep through.
